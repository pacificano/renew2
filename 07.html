Our Services

At Pacificano we carefully calculate the carbon emissions produced as a result of our web server being on 24 hours a day. We then offset these emissions by planting trees at Tree Park in Bondi. As the trees we plant are young, and will absorb much more carbon as they grow, we will, in the long term, be a carbon-positive company.

We can therefore have a more positive environmental impact than companies that use renewable energy, as well as providing a better value for money service. Read more about our eco credentials.
